I'm Craig Akutagawa, and this site is my personal space for writing down what I am building, learning, and revisiting.

Right now this page is intentionally lightweight. It gives the site a clear anchor before the content library grows.

## What I plan to share

- project notes and build logs
- lessons from programming and debugging
- longer reflections when a topic deserves more than a quick post

## Why keep it simple

A personal blog works best when publishing is easy. This starter avoids heavy structure so new posts can be added quickly and the design can evolve over time.
