Welcome to My Blog

This site starts simple on purpose.

The first version focuses on the basics: a clear home page, an about page, and a place to publish posts without fighting the tooling. That makes it easier to build a writing habit before adding more features.

Over time, this blog can expand to include:

  • technical notes
  • project write-ups
  • small experiments worth documenting

For now, the important part is that the structure is in place and new posts are easy to add.
